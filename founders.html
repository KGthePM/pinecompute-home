Kyle's Story

I'm Kyle, the founder of PineCompute. My path to AI started unconventionally: studying neuroscience at Boston University, I became obsessed with how brains retrieve information‚Äîhow context triggers memory, how associations form, how decision-making actually works. That curiosity shaped everything that came after.

When I started building AI systems, I realized that retrieval-augmented generation wasn't just clever engineering‚Äîit was mimicking how human memory actually functions. And I realized something else: if I understand how minds work, I can build AI that respects how businesses work. So I built locally. No cloud vendor lock-in. No external dependencies. Just PrivateGPT, Ollama, and a 16GB Linux PC, solving real problems.

Over the years I've built everything from trading systems to survival apps to multiplayer games. But every single project taught me the same lesson: the best AI is the AI you control. Not the AI you rent from a vendor. The AI that lives on your hardware, touches only your data, and answers to you.

"Small businesses deserve AI superpowers without vendor lock-in or privacy compromises. That's what we're building at PineCompute."

Today, alongside co-founder David Rainear, I'm building PineCompute Core and other PineCompute‚Äîproducts that turn your private files into an expert assistant that never leaves your server. Because privacy isn't a premium feature. It's a foundation.

This isn't just another AI company. We're building something different: a philosophy where privacy, sovereignty, and intelligence coexist. Where you don't have to sacrifice control to get superpowers.
